Job Title: Senior Data Scientist
Company: FinEdge Technologies
Location: London, UK (Hybrid – 3 days on-site)
Salary: £75,000 – £95,000 + bonus + equity

About the Role
FinEdge Technologies is building the next generation of algorithmic credit scoring and fraud detection systems. As a Senior Data Scientist, you will own end-to-end model development—from feature engineering through to production deployment—within a cross-functional team of engineers and product managers.

Key Responsibilities
- Design, train, and evaluate machine learning models for credit risk, fraud detection, and customer lifetime value prediction.
- Work with large-scale structured and time-series transaction data (billions of rows) using Spark and distributed SQL.
- Collaborate with MLOps engineers to move models from Jupyter notebooks into production pipelines on AWS SageMaker.
- Conduct rigorous A/B testing and causal inference analyses to measure business impact of model changes.
- Mentor junior data scientists and review pull requests.
- Present findings to the CFO and Head of Risk in clear, non-technical language.

Required Skills & Experience
- 4+ years of industry experience in a data science or quantitative analyst role.
- Expert-level Python: pandas, NumPy, scikit-learn, XGBoost, LightGBM.
- Strong knowledge of statistical modelling: logistic regression, survival analysis, Bayesian inference.
- Experience with SQL (BigQuery or Redshift preferred) and Spark/PySpark for large-scale data processing.
- Solid understanding of model validation: cross-validation, stratified sampling, AUC-ROC, Gini coefficient, PSI.
- Hands-on experience with MLflow or similar experiment tracking tools.
- Familiarity with Docker and basic CI/CD practices (GitHub Actions or Jenkins).
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills.

Desirable Skills
- Experience in regulated financial environments (FCA, Basel III context).
- Knowledge of interpretable ML (SHAP, LIME) for model explainability.
- Familiarity with Kubernetes for model serving.
- Prior experience with graph neural networks or network analysis for fraud.
- AWS certifications (Solutions Architect or Machine Learning Specialty).

Tech Stack
Python, PySpark, BigQuery, AWS SageMaker, MLflow, Docker, GitHub Actions, Tableau

Equal Opportunity
FinEdge Technologies is committed to building a diverse team. We encourage applications from all backgrounds.
