Job Title: NLP / LLM Engineer
Company: Speechmark Labs
Location: Edinburgh, UK (Hybrid)
Salary: £70,000 – £90,000

About the Role
Speechmark Labs builds conversational AI products for legal tech and professional services. We need an NLP Engineer who is deeply familiar with transformer architectures and has hands-on experience fine-tuning and deploying LLMs in production. You will work on contract understanding, legal document summarisation, and question-answering systems.

Key Responsibilities
- Fine-tune and evaluate large language models (GPT-4, Claude, Llama-3, Mistral) for domain-specific tasks including legal document understanding and entity extraction.
- Design and implement RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) pipelines using vector databases (Pinecone, Chroma, FAISS) and embedding models.
- Evaluate LLM outputs: hallucination detection, factual consistency scoring (BERTScore, ROUGE, faithfulness metrics).
- Build production NLP pipelines for named entity recognition (NER), relation extraction, and coreference resolution.
- Implement prompt engineering strategies, chain-of-thought prompting, and few-shot learning patterns.
- Optimise inference costs: quantisation (GPTQ, AWQ), vLLM serving, batching strategies.
- Work with annotators to design labelling schemas and measure inter-annotator agreement (Cohen's Kappa).

Required Skills & Experience
- 3+ years building production NLP systems.
- Deep understanding of transformer architectures (attention mechanisms, tokenisation, positional encodings).
- Proficiency in Python and HuggingFace ecosystem: Transformers, Datasets, PEFT, Evaluate.
- Experience with LangChain, LlamaIndex, or similar orchestration frameworks.
- Hands-on fine-tuning experience: LoRA/QLoRA, instruction tuning, RLHF concepts.
- Strong grounding in NLP evaluation: BLEU, ROUGE, BERTScore, RAGAs.
- Experience with vector databases: Pinecone, Weaviate, FAISS, or Chroma.
- Proficient with PyTorch.

Desirable Skills
- Experience with multimodal models (vision-language models: LLaVA, CLIP).
- Knowledge of legal or professional services domain.
- Contributions to open-source NLP projects.
- Experience with speech recognition (Whisper, wav2vec) or TTS systems.
- Understanding of Constitutional AI, RLHF, and alignment techniques.
- Academic background in computational linguistics or ML.

Tech Stack
Python, PyTorch, HuggingFace, LangChain, FAISS, Pinecone, vLLM, AWS, FastAPI
